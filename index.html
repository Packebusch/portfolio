About me

As a theoretical physicist by training I am always curious, asking loads of questions and trying to find out what the real problem is.

The best insight I ever got from a colleague was that coming up with answers is easy, what's hard is asking the right questions.

I figured that she was right and this shaped my work ever since. Curiosity, fun and collaboration with others is key for me. Therefore I am drawn to the cooperative principles of ownership, collaboration and autonomy. For me, leadership is the genius of helping people to achieve more together.
